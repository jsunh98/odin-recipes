Crock Pot Italian Beef Recipe


Crock Pot Italian Beef

  • Servings: 15-20
  • Prep Time: < 20 min
  • Cook Time: 8 hrs

This is one of my favorite recipes. It's really easy to make and is a hit at pot lucks or family dinners.

The ingredient list below is a very loose list with the ability to replace certain ingredients to your liking. For instance, My recipe includes using fresh herbs because I think that it adds to the fresh flavor of the beef. Feel free to use dried herbs as a replacement. If you do use dried herbs, make sure to use less than the servings I suggest. I would probably cut the amount in half for dried herbs. This recipe will need a 6qt Crock Pot/Slow Cooker or Pressure Cooker and should serve about 15-20C people, or maybe 3 of me.


Also, my recipe will vary most conventional recipies for Italian Beef because I add the element of Toasted Garlic Bread w/Cheese. It's a game changer imo.

Ingredients:

  • 4 lbs Chuck Roast, or a leaner cut if you'd like
  • 4 tbsp EVOO
  • 2 qts Beef Broth
  • 2 pouches of dried italian dressing mix
  • 1 bunch of fresh basil
  • 1 bunch of fresh parsley
  • 1 bunch of fresh oregano
  • 3-4 tspn minced garlic or garlic powder
  • 1 tub of garlic butter
  • 15-20 hoagie rolls
  • 24-48oz of shredded provolone cheese
  • 16oz of shredded parmesan cheese
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 jar of sliced Pepperoncini
  • 1 jar of Giardiniera
